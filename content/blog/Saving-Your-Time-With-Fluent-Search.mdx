## Fluent Search: The Ultimate Search Companion

Fluent Search is a tool that allows you to quickly find and access various resources on your computer, including active processes, in-app elements, files, and web searches. It supports keyboard-based navigation, customizable interfaces, and search tags to filter results efficiently.
